LA CASA

DE PAPEL

Money Heist, also known as Casa de Papel in Spanish, is one of the biggest drama series on Netflix. If you haven’t watched this series yet, you need to watch it right now!

In my humble opinion, I think the story line and the actors are phenomenal! I truly enjoy watching all the twists and turns that keep me at the edge of my seat. The writers of the series have done such a great job developing the characters that I think I am able to shed some light on each of the character’s zodiac signs (their sun signs).
